What is HDD?

HDD is the device, in which stored all information from computer. The main parts of HDDs is the disks, reading/writing heads and mechanical parts. Reading/writing speed ranges from 50 Mb/s until 120 Mb/s. Information storage is done through the creation of a magnetic field.

What is SSD?

SSD is also storage device. It keeps information on the flash memory, which allows to get reading/writing speed upper then 250Mb/s. It can be said, that its doesn't belongs to the class of HDDs, because there is no disks, in which stores all informstion and magnetic head, wherewith reading or writing information. It doesn't have mechanical parts, so it runs silently.

The difference

In addition to the silently working and information's reading/writing speed, SSD has smaller sensitivity to the magnetic field effects, it's have longer liftime, less power consumption. The OS is booting up faster up to 3-4 times.
